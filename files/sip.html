Understanding SIP compounding

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) lets you invest a fixed amount in a mutual fund every month. The power of SIPs comes from rupee cost averaging — you buy more units when markets are low and fewer when they're high.

The step-up feature reflects increasing your SIP by a fixed percentage each year (e.g. 10% annually), which can significantly boost your final corpus as your income grows.
