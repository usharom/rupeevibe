💰 Debt Fund Vs FD Return Comparison Calculator

In a Fixed Deposit, your growth is slowed down because you pay tax on the interest earned every single year, even if you don't touch the money.


Debt funds work differently: you only pay tax when you choose to sell. This allows the money that would have gone toward taxes to stay invested, earning you more interest on your interest and creating a much larger wealth pool over time.


Use this calculator to compare how yearly taxation impacts FD returns versus debt funds that grow without annual tax drag.
