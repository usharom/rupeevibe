About home loan EMIs

Your EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment) is calculated using the standard reducing balance method. Each EMI repays both interest and principal — in early months, most of the EMI goes towards interest; as the loan progresses, the principal component rises.

Making even small annual prepayments can dramatically reduce your total interest outgo and shorten your loan tenure. Try entering a prepayment amount above to see the impact.
