About RupeeVibe

India-focused financial calculators built to help you plan with more clarity and less guesswork.

Why RupeeVibe exists

RupeeVibe was built out of frustration. Most free financial calculators online are designed for Western markets — they assume different tax structures, inflation rates, and investment vehicles that simply don't apply to Indian households.

We wanted a simple, honest set of tools that any Indian saver could use — whether you're a first-time investor figuring out your SIP amount, a homeowner estimating your EMI, or someone planning for retirement 20 years away.

All our calculators are free, require no sign-up, and run entirely in your browser. We hope they help you make better decisions with your money.

Built for India

Assumptions and examples that feel relevant for Indian users, not copied from overseas templates.

Simple to use

No clutter, no accounts, and no complicated setup — just fast answers when you need them.

Privacy first

Your numbers stay in your browser. We do not ask you to hand over personal financial data.

Disclaimer: RupeeVibe provides calculators for educational and planning purposes only. Results are estimates based on the inputs you provide and assumed rates of return. This is not financial advice. Please consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
