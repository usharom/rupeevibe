How this calculator works

We calculate the corpus you need at retirement by projecting your current monthly expenses forward to retirement age using the inflation rate you enter. We then figure out the lump sum needed to fund your entire post-retirement life.

We then project your current savings and future monthly contributions forward using your expected return rate, and subtract any big-ticket expenses along the way.

The gap between what you'll have and what you need gives you your savings shortfall (or surplus).
